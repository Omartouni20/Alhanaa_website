About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Al Hanaa for Agricultural Development is a pioneering force in Egypt’s agricultural sector. Strategically located at Kilometer 132 on the Asyut Western Desert Road, our operations benefit from fertile lands and ideal growing conditions that enable year-round cultivation and sustainable growth. We are proud to be part of the El Morshedy Group, a trusted name with over four decades of experience across multiple industries including commodity trading and flour milling. As one of three flagship companies under the group’s umbrella, Al Hanaa operates alongside El Morshedy Trading and El Safa for Grains, drawing on their vast expertise to strengthen our capabilities and drive our vision forward.

What We Do

At Al Hanaa, we specialize in cultivating premium fruits and seasonal crops while expanding into value-added products like pickled olives and raisin grapes. Our vertically integrated model allows us to oversee the entire production cycle—from farming and processing to packaging and export—ensuring complete traceability, consistency, and superior quality. In partnership with our logistics affiliate, Green Oasis, we efficiently manage supply chain operations, enabling timely delivery to both local and global markets. Our export success, particularly in Europe, has earned us a reputation as a reliable name in international agriculture.
