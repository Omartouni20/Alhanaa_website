About

Our Story

Al Hanaa for Agricultural Development is a leading enterprise in Egypt’s agricultural sector, dedicated to modern, sustainable farming practices and high-quality crop production. Founded in 2010 and strategically located at Kilometer 132 on the Asyut Western Desert Road, our operations benefit from fertile land and optimal growing conditions that support year-round cultivation and long-term agricultural planning. As part of the El Morshedy Group, Al Hanaa is one of three distinguished companies under the group’s umbrella, with an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable growth within the agricultural industry.

Part of El Morshedy Group
Al Hanaa is proud to be part of the El Morshedy Group, which brings over four decades of experience in industries such as commodity trading and flour milling. Our sister companies, El Morshedy Trading and El Safa for Grains, lend their vast expertise to enhance our operations, helping us maintain a strong, stable foundation for growth.

What We Do

At Al Hanaa, we specialized in cultivating high-quality fruits and seasonal crops while also expanding into the production of processed items, such as pickled olives and raisin grapes. Our vertically integrated approach allows us to control every stage of production—from farming and processing to export—ensuring superior products that exceed customer expectations. With the support of our sister company, Green Oasis, we manage logistics and streamline operations, ensuring timely delivery of products to both local and international markets. Our success in exports, particularly to Germany, has cemented our reputation as a trusted name in global agriculture.

Sustainability at Our Core

We are passionate about promoting sustainable agriculture.
